Recognise any of these?

The symptoms of a disconnected stack.

📋

Data lives in multiple places

The same client is in your CRM, your project tool, and a spreadsheet — and they're never quite in sync. Someone is always the source of truth and they hate it.

🔁

Manual handoffs between tools

A deal closes → someone manually creates a project → someone else sends the welcome email → someone updates the tracker. Three humans doing one job.

📊

Reporting takes hours to pull

The weekly report requires pulling data from three places, reformatting it in a spreadsheet, and hoping nothing's been updated since you started. Every. Single. Week.

🧠

Ops knowledge lives in heads

When someone leaves, a process breaks. Because the process isn't documented anywhere — it's just what they knew to do. That's fragile at scale.
